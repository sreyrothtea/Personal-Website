About Me

关于我

I work at the intersection of language, government, and international communication. Over the past decade, I have supported senior government officials, coordinated national-level initiatives, and contributed to cross-cultural and research-driven projects.

我的工作处于语言、政府事务与国际传播的交汇领域。过去十余年间， 我曾协助政府高级官员开展工作，参与国家级项目协调，并积累了丰富的跨文化与研究项目经验。

My professional journey has taken me from education and community programs to high-level government environments, where precision, discretion, and effective communication are essential.

我的职业发展始于教育与社区项目，并逐渐进入高标准、高要求的政府工作环境， 在实践中不断深化我对沟通、责任与专业精神的理解。

In 2025, I was awarded the MOFCOM Scholarship to pursue a Master’s degree in Journalism and Communication (Digital Communication) at the Communication University of China, Beijing (2025–2027).

2025年，我获得中国商务部奖学金，将于2025年至2027年在北京中国传媒大学攻读 新闻与传播学（数字传播方向）硕士学位。
